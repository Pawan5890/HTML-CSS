Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a technology that allows machines to think and learn like humans. It is used in smartphones, voice assistants, online shopping, and even self-driving cars. AI helps in solving complex problems quickly and improves efficiency in many industries such as healthcare, education, and banking.

In the future, AI will continue to grow and make our lives easier. However, it is important to use AI responsibly and ethically to ensure it benefits society.
